OHO Bike Share

Branding and website design for a bikeshare company

For this project, I developed a logo and established branding guidelines for a bikeshare company, complemented by the creation of promotional posters and billboards. The website design prioritised a mobile-first approach and emphasised delivering the Minimum Viable Product (MVP). Bootstrap 5 was used for the implementation of the website.
