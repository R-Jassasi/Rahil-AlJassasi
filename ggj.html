Global Game Jam 23

3D modeling and product filming

THE CUBE is a concept 3D design of a portable safe (fictional) that was modelled and rendered using Autodesk Maya, then 3D printed. A short promotional video was shot with my classmates to showcase how to use the safe.

The Cube Renders


Ideation Process

How the project came to life

While I was squeezing my brain for more than two days trying to get an idea for this project, I remember trying to solve my Rubik cube that I had unsolved for years. I thought about how hard it is to solve it until you understand the mathematical concept of it and practice a lot. I don’t know how exactly the idea came, but I thought, what if this hard puzzle is useful for something else… like a safe? A secret, mini one?

3D Printing The Cube


After modeling and printing The Cube, we created a script and storyboards to film a product explination video for the printed cube. Then we shot the video in a small studio and recorded the narrative to put everything together for the short video.
