Global Game Jam 23+24

UWE Foundry global game event

I have participated in GGJ in 2023 and again in 2024. Each with a different brilliant team that helped make our idea come -almost, as best as it can be in 3 days- true! In 2023 I was the head of graphic design team and helped create game assets and menus. In 2024, I had a more technical role and helped create the game map and puzzles. In both of them, I've had major contribution in creating the concept, scripts and story narratives.
